# Editorial Guidelines
**Status:** Active  
**Supersedes:** Nothing — complements `CONTENT_TONE.md` (tone) with operational rules (what, how much, how)  
**Applies to:** All content published on georgeungureanu.doctor

---

## Foundation

These guidelines extend the voice principles in `docs/content/CONTENT_TONE.md`. Read that document first. This document adds the operational rules: word counts, heading hierarchy, internal linking quotas, CTA standards, and the distinction between what a content writer produces and what a medical reviewer approves.

---

## Content Lengths by Type

| Content type | Body word count | Summary | FAQ answers |
|--------------|----------------|---------|-------------|
| Article (articol) | 900–1,800 | 40–60 | 40–100 each |
| Medical Condition (afecțiune) | 600–1,200 (across all wysiwyg fields) | 40–60 | 30–80 each |
| Surgical Procedure (intervenție) | 700–1,400 (across all wysiwyg fields) | 40–60 | 40–100 each |

**Why upper limits:** Exhaustive content can read as filler. A confident, well-edited 1,000-word article serves patients better than a padded 2,500-word one.

## Heading Hierarchy

| Level | Use case | Rules |
|-------|----------|-------|
| H1 | Post title (set by WordPress/Elementor) | Never manually placed in body content |
| H2 | Major content sections | Maximum 6 per article; each should stand alone as a scanning point |
| H3 | Sub-sections within H2 | FAQ questions use H3; maximum 3 H3 per H2 section |
| H4 | Rarely needed | Use only for deeply nested content; avoid in patient-facing body |

**Rule:** Every H2 should be answerable with "The patient reads this heading and knows what they're about to learn."

## Internal Linking Standards

### Quotas per content piece

| Content type | Min links to conditions | Min links to procedures | Min links to articles |
|--------------|------------------------|------------------------|-----------------------|
| Article | 1 | 1 | 0 (auto-generated by shortcode) |
| Condition | 0 | 1 | 0 (auto-generated by shortcode) |
| Procedure | 1 | 0 | 0 (auto-generated by shortcode) |

### Link placement
- Link on the most informative word or phrase — not "click here" or "this page"
- Link conditions and procedures on first mention in body text
- Do not link the same target more than twice in the same article
- The `[gu_articles_for_post]` shortcode handles article cross-links automatically; do not duplicate them manually

### Anchor text examples
- Correct: `[hernie de disc lombară](/afectiuni/hernie-de-disc-lombara/)`
- Incorrect: `[citește mai mult](/afectiuni/hernie-de-disc-lombara/)`
- Incorrect: `[accesați pagina afecțiunii](/afectiuni/hernie-de-disc-lombara/)`

## CTA Standards

Every piece of content must end with a CTA that invites the patient to take the next step. The `/programari/` page is the destination.

### Approved CTA patterns

**For articles (general):**
> Titlu: "Aveți întrebări despre această afecțiune?"  
> Text: "Dr. George Ungureanu oferă consultații pentru evaluarea personalizată a simptomelor dumneavoastră. Primul pas este o programare."  
> Buton: "Programați o consultație"

**For condition pages:**
> Titlu: "Simptomele vă îngrijorează?"  
> Text: "O evaluare timpurie oferă mai multe opțiuni de tratament. Programați o consultație cu Dr. Ungureanu pentru a discuta situația dumneavoastră specifică."  
> Buton: "Programați o consultație"

**For procedure pages:**
> Titlu: "Aflați dacă această intervenție este potrivită pentru dumneavoastră"  
> Text: "Eligibilitatea pentru orice procedură chirurgicală se stabilește individual, în urma consultației și a investigațiilor imagistice. Luați legătura cu cabinetul pentru a programa o evaluare."  
> Buton: "Programați o consultație"

### Prohibited in CTAs
- "Programați-vă acum și vindecați-vă!"
- "Nu așteptați — fiecare zi contează!"
- "Cel mai bun neurochirurg din România"
- Any outcome promise

## Medical Disclaimer Requirement

Every article must contain the following disclaimer (or an equivalent approved by Dr. Ungureanu). Placement: end of body content, before the CTA section.

**Standard disclaimer (Romanian):**
> *Informațiile din acest articol au caracter educativ general și nu înlocuiesc consultul medical de specialitate. Fiecare pacient are un tablou clinic unic, iar recomandările de tratament sunt stabilite individual, în urma evaluării medicale. Dacă aveți simptome, consultați un medic.*

This disclaimer is **mandatory** for all articles and conditions. For procedures, it may be adapted to:
> *Informațiile de pe această pagină sunt orientative. Indicarea și efectuarea oricărei intervenții chirurgicale se stabilesc individual, în urma consultației preoperatorii cu Dr. Ungureanu și pe baza investigațiilor imagistice complete.*

## Numbers and Statistics

1. Always give a source or framing context for statistics (e.g., "conform literaturii medicale," "studiile arată că," "în experiența clinică")
2. Prefer ratios and frequencies over percentages when talking to patients ("9 din 10 pacienți" beats "90% din cazuri" for comprehension)
3. Recovery timelines: always give a range, not a single figure ("4–6 săptămâni," not "o lună")
4. Never cite statistics that cannot be substantiated — when in doubt, omit the number and state the direction ("marea majoritate a pacienților")

---

## Romanian Language Rules

1. Use formal register: **"dumneavoastră"** not "tu" (patients are addressed formally throughout)
2. Use medical terms followed by Romanian equivalents on first use
3. Use "neurochirurg" not "chirurg creier" or colloquial alternatives
4. Diacritics are required: ă, â, î, ș, ț (with comma below, not cedilla: ș ț not ş ţ)
5. Numbers below 10: write in full ("trei săptămâni," not "3 săptămâni") — except in bulleted lists, timelines, or technical specifications
6. Dates in body text: "15 mai 2026" format; in ACF fields: YYYY-MM-DD

## What AI Can Draft vs. What Requires Human Review

| Content element | AI can draft | Requires Dr. Ungureanu review |
|-----------------|-------------|-------------------------------|
| Article structure and outline | Yes | Structure only (no medical claims) |
| Patient-facing explanations | Yes (first draft) | Yes — all medical claims |
| Symptom lists | Yes | Yes |
| Diagnosis descriptions | Yes | Yes |
| Treatment recommendations | Yes (general framing) | Yes — especially surgical candidacy criteria |
| Risk descriptions | Yes (general) | Yes — must be accurate for this practice |
| Recovery timelines | Yes (general ranges) | Yes — Dr. Ungureanu may have specific protocols |
| FAQ answers | Yes | Yes |
| CTA copy | Yes | No (editorial approval sufficient) |
| SEO title/description | Yes | No (editorial approval sufficient) |
| Medical disclaimer | Use approved template | Do not modify without Dr. Ungureanu sign-off |

## Update Frequency

| Content type | Review cycle | Trigger for earlier update |
|--------------|-------------|---------------------------|
| Articles | Every 12 months | New research changes recommendations |
| Conditions | Every 18 months | New diagnostic criteria or treatment options |
| Procedures | Every 18 months | New technique or equipment adopted by practice |

Update date is tracked via the `medical_review_date` ACF field (group_ar) or an admin note for conditions/procedures.
